Understanding the Logic

This calculator uses the Atwater Factors to determine your grams. In nutritional science, energy density is measured as follows (Rolls, 2017):

  • Protein: 4 kcal per gram
  • Carbohydrates: 4 kcal per gram
  • Fats: 9 kcal per gram

The protein target is set at approximately 2g per kg of body weight (using a 2.2 lbs-to-kg conversion), which is a common recommendation for preserving muscle during weight loss (Hector & Phillips, 2018). The remaining calories are split between fats (25%) and carbohydrates, falling within the Acceptable Macronutrient Distribution Ranges (AMDR) (Gatehouse, 2005).
